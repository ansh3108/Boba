    Extra Travel Tips

    • Always carry a reusable water bottle to stay hydrated.
    • Learn a few local phrases to connect with people.
    • Keep emergency cash in a separate place.
    • Use packing cubes to organize your luggage.
    • Take photos of important documents before your trip.
    • Try local foods and step out of your comfort zone.
    • Respect local customs and traditions.
    • Download offline maps and guides.
    • Bring a portable charger for your devices.
    • Wake up early to enjoy popular spots without crowds.
