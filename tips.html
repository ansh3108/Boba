Travel tips

Hacks to make your next trip more smarter!

  • Pack Light: You won't wear half the clothes you bring anyway!
  • Use Google Maps Offline: Download maps before leaving Wi-Fi!
  • Roll, Don't fold!: Saves space and avoids wrinkles!
  • Local SIMs: Get cheap data plans in the country you're visiting!
  • Keep Digital Copies: Store passport and IDs in your email or cloud!
  • Early flights= Less Chaos: Trust me, the airport's chill in the AM.
